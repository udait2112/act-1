Facts about dogs

Dogs can smell time and emotions because their noses read chemical changes in the air. They can sense storms long before humans, understand hundreds of words, and even pick up your mood just from your tone. Every dog has a nose-print as unique as a fingerprint, and that cute head tilt? better.
